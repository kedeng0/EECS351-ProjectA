Welcome to Space World!
Following are the commands you can try:

Press W and S to move your Lunar Rover, Mouse drag to move your Rocket

Press p to pause and unpause animation, Press Q and E to control camera on Lunar Rover

Click on Driver slower or faster the change speed of the Lunar Rover

Click on Reset to restart the animation

Click anywhere to change the size of your Lunar Rover
